About Me

My Mission

To empower farmers and agribusinesses in Nigeria with practical, science-driven solutions that enhance productivity, profitability, and resilience.

My Journey

I trained as a Veterinary Doctor (DVM) at the University of Maiduguri, grounding me in animal health, welfare, and best practices.

Later, I earned my MBA, equipping me with strategic leadership and business acumen needed to scale agricultural ventures.

Since 2018, I've led Hyelclar Agroentrepreneur, driving growth in poultry farming, fishery, crop production, and consultancy services.

I believe that agriculture in Nigeria can flourish when research, integrity, and effective business strategy meet. My work is dedicated to making that possible.

Core Expertise

As CEO of Hyelclar, I wear many hats — strategist, mentor, innovator, and implementer. Below are key areas I lead and specialize in:

Agro-Business Strategy & Consultancy

I design and implement tailored farm systems, operations plans, and management frameworks for scaling, risk mitigation, and yield optimization.

Integrated Farm Production

Overseeing livestock, poultry, fishery, and crop value chains—from feed and animal care to harvesting, processing, and distribution.

Project Development & Innovation

I spearhead projects such as high-capacity broiler farms, mobile fish ponds, waste-to-resource systems, and precision planting initiatives.

Capacity Building & Training

I lead training and mentorship programs for aspiring farmers—translating complex agritech methods into practical, replicable models.
